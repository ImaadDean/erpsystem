What is IDURAR?

IDURAR is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed to streamline your business operations. Built with modern technologies, it provides an intuitive interface for managing customers, invoices, quotes, payments, and more.

Key Features

Customer Management

Complete customer database with contact information and history

Invoice Management

Create, send, and track invoices with automated workflows

Quote Management

Professional quotes and proposals with conversion tracking

Payment Processing

Multiple payment methods and automated payment tracking
